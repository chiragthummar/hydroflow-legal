Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log water?
Tap any of the quick log buttons on the home screen (150ml, 250ml, 350ml, 500ml) to instantly log water. You can also tap the "+" button in the tab bar for more options including different beverages.
How is my daily goal calculated?
Your personalized goal is based on your body weight, age, gender, and activity level entered during onboarding. Premium users get AI-powered smart goals that also factor in weather and exercise data.
How does the garden work?
Every time you drink water, your active plants grow. Plants progress through 5 stages: Seed, Sprout, Growing, Blooming, and Full. If you stop drinking water for 48+ hours, your plants start wilting. You can grow up to 3 plants at once.
Why am I not receiving reminders?
Make sure notifications are enabled for Water Reminder Tracker App in your iPhone Settings > Notifications > Water Reminder Tracker App. Also check that reminders are configured in the app's Settings > Reminders. Ensure Do Not Disturb or Focus modes aren't blocking notifications.
How do I connect Apple Health?
Go to Settings in the app and look for HealthKit integration. The app will request permission to read fitness data and write water intake. You can also manage permissions in iPhone Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > Water Reminder Tracker App.
What's included in Premium?
Premium includes AI-powered smart goals, full history with weekly/monthly/yearly charts, all 10 garden plants, Apple Watch app, custom beverages, all widget styles, data export, smart location/weather reminders, and health correlation insights.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Open iPhone Settings > tap your Apple ID > Subscriptions > Water Reminder Tracker App > Cancel Subscription. You'll retain premium access until the end of your current billing period. You can also manage subscriptions at apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.
How do I restore my purchase?
Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases". This will sync your subscription status with the App Store. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
Can I request a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find the Water Reminder Tracker App purchase, and select "Request a Refund".
Is my data backed up?
All data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud Backup enabled, your app data will be included in your device backup. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all data unless you have a backup.
What does the caffeine tracker do?
The caffeine tracker monitors your daily caffeine intake from all logged beverages (coffee, tea, energy drinks, soda). It shows your status as Low, Moderate, or High and provides tips to manage your intake.
