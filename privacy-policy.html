Privacy Policy

HydroFlow - Smart Water Reminder

Last updated: April 4, 2026

HydroFlow ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Health & Fitness Data (Apple HealthKit)

With your explicit permission, HydroFlow reads and writes the following HealthKit data:

  • Reads: Body weight, workout data, sleep analysis, active energy burned, heart rate, resting heart rate
  • Writes: Dietary water intake

Important: HealthKit data is stored exclusively on your device and in your personal iCloud Health data. HydroFlow does not transmit, sell, share, or use HealthKit data for advertising or marketing purposes. HealthKit data is never shared with third parties.

1.2 Health Tracking Data

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Hydration logs (beverages, amounts, timestamps)
  • Medicine schedules and dose logs
  • Meal plans and food logs
  • Fasting window configurations
  • Caffeine intake records
  • Liquid and beverage preferences

1.3 Location Data

If you enable location-based smart reminders, the app accesses your location solely to trigger hydration reminders at configured places (gym, office, outdoors). Location data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

1.4 User Preferences

Body metrics (weight, age, gender, activity level), daily hydration goals, reminder schedules, appearance settings, and notification preferences are stored locally on your device.

1.5 Analytics & Crash Data

We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to improve app quality:

  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymized usage events (e.g., feature usage, app opens). No personally identifiable information is included.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports including device type, OS version, and stack traces to help us fix bugs. No personal health data is included in crash reports.

You can opt out of analytics in the app's Settings.

1.6 Purchase Data

Subscription and purchase transactions are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect or store payment information such as credit card numbers. We only receive confirmation of subscription status from Apple.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide core app functionality (hydration tracking, reminders, goal calculation)
  • To personalize your hydration goals based on body metrics, activity, and weather
  • To trigger smart reminders based on location, weather, and activity context
  • To display your hydration history and health correlation insights
  • To manage your subscription and premium features
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs (via anonymized analytics)

3. Data Storage

All personal and health data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. HydroFlow does not operate cloud servers and does not sync your data to any external database. Your data stays on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Apple HealthKit - Health data sync (on-device only)
  • Apple StoreKit - In-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Apple WeatherKit - Weather data for smart goal adjustments
  • Firebase Analytics - Anonymized usage analytics (Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase Crashlytics - Crash reporting (Privacy Policy)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Anonymized analytics data is processed by Firebase (Google) solely for app improvement purposes. No health data, HealthKit data, or personally identifiable information is shared with any third party.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app at any time.
  • Export: Premium users can export their hydration data as CSV.
  • Deletion: Deleting the app removes all locally stored data. You can also delete individual entries within the app.
  • Analytics Opt-Out: You can disable analytics in Settings at any time.
  • HealthKit: You can revoke HealthKit permissions in iOS Settings > Privacy > Health at any time.
  • Location: You can revoke location permissions in iOS Settings > Privacy > Location Services at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

HydroFlow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. GDPR (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, and data portability. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. To exercise your rights, contact us at the email below.

9. CCPA (California Users)

California residents have the right to know what data is collected, request deletion, and opt out of data sales. We do not sell personal data. All data is stored on-device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
