Privacy Policy

HydroFlow - Smart Water Reminder

Last updated: April 9, 2026

HydroFlow ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Health & Fitness Data (Apple HealthKit)

With your explicit permission, HydroFlow reads and writes the following HealthKit data:

  • Reads: Body weight, workout data, sleep analysis, active energy burned, heart rate, resting heart rate
  • Writes: Dietary water intake

Important: HealthKit data is stored exclusively on your device and in your personal iCloud Health data. HydroFlow does not transmit, sell, share, or use HealthKit data for advertising or marketing purposes. HealthKit data is never shared with third parties, including analytics or crash-reporting services.

1.2 Health Tracking Data

The app stores the following data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Hydration logs (beverages, amounts, timestamps)
  • Medicine schedules, dose logs, and adherence records
  • Meal plans, food logs, and nutritional summaries
  • Fasting window configurations and tracking
  • Caffeine and liquid intake records
  • Beverage preferences and custom drink types
  • Virtual garden progress and plant growth data
  • Streak and achievement data

1.3 Location Data

If you enable location-based smart reminders, the app accesses your location solely to:

  • Trigger hydration reminders when you arrive at configured places (gym, office, outdoors)
  • Retrieve local weather data via Apple WeatherKit for temperature- and humidity-based hydration goal adjustments

Location data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers. You can disable location access at any time in iOS Settings.

1.4 Weather Data

HydroFlow uses Apple WeatherKit to retrieve current temperature and humidity for your location. This data is used exclusively to adjust your daily hydration goal based on weather conditions. Weather requests are made directly to Apple's servers and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

1.5 User Preferences

Body metrics (weight, age, gender, activity level), daily hydration goals, reminder schedules, appearance settings, notification preferences, and accessibility settings are stored locally on your device.

1.6 Analytics & Crash Data

We use Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Firebase Remote Config to improve app quality:

  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymized usage events (e.g., feature usage, app opens). No personally identifiable information or health data is included.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports including device type, OS version, and stack traces to help us fix bugs. No personal health data is included in crash reports.
  • Firebase Remote Config: Delivers feature flags and app configuration remotely. No personal data is sent.

You can opt out of analytics at any time in the app's Settings. When opted out, no analytics events are sent to Firebase.

1.7 Purchase Data

Subscription and purchase transactions are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect or store payment information such as credit card numbers. We only receive confirmation of subscription status (active, expired, or in grace period) from Apple's servers.

1.8 Notification Data

HydroFlow uses local notifications (not push notifications from external servers) for water reminders, meal reminders, and medicine reminders. Notification schedules are stored on-device. No notification data is sent to external servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide core app functionality (hydration tracking, reminders, goal calculation)
  • To personalize your hydration goals based on body metrics, activity, weather, and exercise
  • To trigger smart reminders based on location, weather, and activity context
  • To display your hydration history, trends, and health correlation insights
  • To power the virtual garden gamification feature
  • To manage your subscription and premium features
  • To display widgets, Live Activities, and Apple Watch complications with your progress
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs (via anonymized analytics)

3. Data Storage

All personal and health data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. HydroFlow does not operate cloud servers and does not sync your data to any external database. Your data stays on your device.

Data shared between the main app, widgets, Apple Watch companion app, and Live Activities is kept within a secure App Group container on your device.

4. Apple Watch & Widgets

HydroFlow includes an Apple Watch companion app and home screen/lock screen widgets:

  • Apple Watch: Syncs hydration data between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. Data transfers occur directly between your devices and do not pass through external servers.
  • Widgets & Live Activities: Display your hydration progress on your home screen, lock screen, and Dynamic Island. Data is shared via an on-device App Group container.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Apple HealthKit — Health data sync (on-device only, Apple Privacy)
  • Apple StoreKit — In-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Apple WeatherKit — Weather data for smart goal adjustments
  • Apple WatchConnectivity — iPhone-to-Watch data sync (device-to-device only)
  • Firebase Analytics — Anonymized usage analytics (Firebase Privacy)
  • Firebase Crashlytics — Crash reporting (Firebase Privacy)
  • Firebase Remote Config — Feature flags and app configuration (Firebase Privacy)

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Anonymized analytics data is processed by Firebase (Google) solely for app improvement purposes. No health data, HealthKit data, location data, or personally identifiable information is shared with any third party.

We do not use any advertising frameworks, tracking pixels, or third-party ad networks. The app does not participate in cross-app tracking.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app at any time.
  • Export: Premium users can export their hydration data as CSV.
  • Deletion: Deleting the app removes all locally stored data. You can also delete individual entries within the app.
  • Analytics Opt-Out: You can disable analytics in Settings at any time.
  • HealthKit: You can revoke HealthKit permissions in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health at any time.
  • Location: You can revoke location permissions in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services at any time.
  • Notifications: You can disable notifications in iOS Settings > Notifications > HydroFlow at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

HydroFlow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided data through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

9. GDPR (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) including access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and the right to object. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data. To exercise your rights or for any questions, contact us at the email below.

10. CCPA (California Users)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to know what data is collected, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal data. We do not sell personal data. All data is stored on-device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app or removing individual records within the app.

11. Data Security

All data is stored in Apple's secure SwiftData container on your device. Communication with Apple services (HealthKit, WeatherKit, StoreKit) and Firebase uses industry-standard TLS/HTTPS encryption. We do not store any data on external servers that could be subject to data breaches.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
